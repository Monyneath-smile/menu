What is the difference between delivery and Pick-Up?

If you choose delivery, a foodpanda rider will collect your order from the restaurant and take it to your chosen delivery address. If you choose Pick-Up, you can self-collect your

food directly from the restaurant for extra savings – and to jump to the front of the queue. Pick-Up orders are available for restaurants only.
